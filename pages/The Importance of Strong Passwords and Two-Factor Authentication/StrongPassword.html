The Importance of Strong Passwords and Two-Factor Authentication

In today's digital world, cyber attacks are on rise specially since 2020. and daily thousands of accounts are being hacked. studies have found that 24% americans use simple password like abc123, Password, 123456, Iloveyou, 111111, Qwerty, Admin. which is easily guessable. why it's so important to use strong password and two-factor-authentication? how it secures you? lets start with strong password first.

The Importance of Strong Passwords

Using strong password is immportant to keep your online accounts safe. hackers often perform dictionary attack on their target. in this attack hacker have a dictionary file in which it includes thousands of most used password around the world. and if you have one of the password from the list then hacker will be able to log into your account. make sure you are not using the password we have mentioned above.

The Importance of Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) requires two forms of identification in order to access your account. either it can be one-time-password or a code on your phone which keep chances again and again. two-factor-authentication makes hacker hard to gain access to you account even if he has the password. hacker still won't be able to log into your account.

Tips for Securing Your Online Accounts

  • Use a different password for each of your online accounts

    • using a different password is a good way to protect your accounts from other platforms if hacker already has found the password of one of your account. in most cases hacker often tries to go use the same password on target's other account. so it is very important to use strong and different password everywhere

  • Avoid using personal information, such as your name or date of birth, in your password

    • if the dictionary attack fails. attacker will try to gain your personal information through your social media account or anywhere possible. like your name, your family members name, your car number, phone number, date of birth and anything personal as possible. and through the informations they've collected. they use it to create new dictionary by using powerful tools. and there will be higher chances for hacker to gain access on your account through this word list. so make sure not to add any personal information in your password.

  • Make your password at least 12 characters long and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols

    • using strong password which includes all the requirements as mentioned above can make it nearly impossible for hacker to gain access to your account using attacks like dictionary or brute force.

  • Use two-factor authentication whenever possible

    • As we talked about the importance of using two-factor-authentication previously. make sure to enable it if you have the option available on any platform you have account on.

  • Keep your software and devices up to date with the latest security updates

    • digital companies often releases update for their product. in update they patch the known vulnerability which can be used by attacker to gain unauthorized access to your account and can steal private informations like password. so it is very immportant to keep your applications up to date.

  • Be cautious when clicking on links or downloading attachments from unknown sources

    • attackers often known to use Social engineering attacks to trick you into clicking on the link they send and entering your informations like password into it. it often looks like a legit link and legit website sent by real source. but its might not be. so its always the best when you get email asking you to change your password of account or anything like that. go to the website yourself by searching it on browser instead of clicking on the link sent by suspicious emails.

By following these simple tips and using strong passwords and two-factor authentication, you can help keep your online accounts and personal information secure.
